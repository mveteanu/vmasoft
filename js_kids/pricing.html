Questions and Answers about Membership

Do I need a paid membership to use CodeGuppy?
CodeGuppy is a free platform. Both free and paid members have access to the same great coding platform and essential tutorials.
Why purchase a membership?
Membership is totally optional but highly recommended. Members enjoy additional content and premium game building assets (funnier characters and scene backgrounds) – which increase coding satisfaction and motivation.
Why are you offering a flat fee lifetime membership plan?
The lifetime membership plan is offered as part of the launch of CodeGuppy platform. It is designed to provides tremendous benefits to our customers while helping us raise the capital needed for growth of the platform.
Lifetime membership vs monthly membership
Both membership plans offer the same benefits. If lifetime membership is available at the time you’re trying to purchase a membership plan, we recommend taking advantage of the offer before we may switch to the monthly subscription model.
When you’ll start offering the monthly membership?
We are currently working to implement the infrastructure for this type of membership. Please follow us on Twitter or subscribe to our newsletter to receive notifications about this plan.
Can I use CodeGuppy to teach coding in class?
Yes! We are offering in the free tier the full coding platform and all the essential coding lessons and tutorials. This is appealing to many coding club teachers who can now use the platform in a class environment for teaching JavaScript. Please see the educators section for more details.
